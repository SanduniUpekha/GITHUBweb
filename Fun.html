FUND RAISING

Annually LC organizes a series of fundraising activities, including raffles, car washes, concerts and door-to-door fundraising campaigns.
Among the recent additions is the sale of Christmas decorations and the Pen Project. So join us!

1.DOOR TO DOOR PROGRAMME

This is another program launched by the Fundraising Division to educate the public about LC activities. Our mission is to raise the living standards of all senior citizens in Sri Lanka. While prioritizing improving the living standards of thousands of affected people, we continue to carry out our programs covering empowerment, counseling, sanitation and livelihood assistance to uplift the living standards of those in need. So join us!

2.TILLS PROJECT

a fundraising business launched through a collection network. While giving priority to improving the living standards of thousands of affected people, we continue our programs covering empowerment, counseling and management, water and sanitation and livelihood assistance to uplift the living standards of those in need. 3.GREETING CARDS

The greeting card project initiated by LC is a major fundraising activity. Proceeds from the project will be used not only to provide assistance to needy seniors in home and day care centers, but also to fund other projects that will help raise the living standards of seniors living with families or alone. About 12 different designs are printed each year. Contact us and buy cards andbecame a part of this service
